Crystal Collector!


You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to the random number given below. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it and it's value is added to your total.


Each time the game restarts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
